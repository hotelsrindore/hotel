About Hotel SR

Your Trusted Stay Partner in Indore

Hotel SR Indore is strategically located opposite Lasudia Police Station on Dewas Naka, offering guests a perfect blend of comfort, safety, and affordability. We pride ourselves on providing clean, well-maintained rooms with modern amenities at honest prices.

Whether you're a business traveler, tourist, or visiting for family functions, our hotel provides the ideal base for exploring Indore. With 24/7 reception, free parking, high-speed WiFi, and attentive staff, we ensure your stay is comfortable and hassle-free.
