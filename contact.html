Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

Check-in and check-out timings?

Check-in is at 12:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability.

Do you provide parking?

Yes, we offer complimentary parking for both cars and bikes for all our guests.

Is food available?

Yes, we have an in-house restaurant. Room service is available from 7 AM to 11 PM.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed. Contact us 24 hours before check-in for a full refund.

Do you host events?

Yes, we have banquet halls for ring ceremonies, parties, and small events.

Is WiFi available?

Yes, complimentary WiFi is available in all rooms and common areas.
